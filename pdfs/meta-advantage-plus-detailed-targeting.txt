# About Advantage+ Detailed Targeting | Meta Ads
## Meta Business Help Center

---

**Important Update:** Detailed targeting exclusions have been removed. You can't use them in new ad sets any more. Removed from active campaigns on March 31, 2025.

---

## What Is Advantage+ Detailed Targeting?

Meta Advantage+ detailed targeting helps improve campaign performance by allowing the system to reach a **broader group of people** than you defined in your detailed targeting selections.

---

## How It Works

1. System looks for better performance opportunities outside your defined audience
2. If found, dynamically expands audience to reflect better performance
3. May expand further to include similar opportunities
4. Shows ad to additional people likely to get more/cheaper results

---

## Key Points

### What It Affects
- ✅ Detailed targeting (interests, behaviors)
- ✅ Audience expansion for better performance

### What It Does NOT Affect
- ❌ Location targeting
- ❌ Age targeting
- ❌ Gender targeting
- ❌ Language targeting

**These continue to apply as set.**

---

## Availability

| Campaign Type | Available |
|---------------|-----------|
| Regular campaigns | ✅ Yes |
| Social issues, elections, politics | ❌ No |

---

## Important Notes

- Estimated audience size won't reflect total expanded audience
- Advertising Standards prohibiting wrongful discrimination still apply
- Targeting options limited for audiences under 18
