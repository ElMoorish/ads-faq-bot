# Getting Started with Meta Ads

## Why Advertise on Meta?

Meta (Facebook & Instagram) reaches over 3.9 billion people across its family of apps.

**Key Benefits:**
- **Massive Reach** - 3.9+ billion users across Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp
- **Precise Targeting** - Demographics, interests, behaviors, custom audiences
- **Multiple Ad Formats** - Images, videos, carousels, collections, stories, reels
- **Objective-based Campaigns** - Awareness, Consideration, Conversion
- **Advanced Analytics** - Comprehensive reporting and insights
- **Cross-platform** - Advertise across all Meta properties

# Campaign Objectives

## AWARENESS Objectives

### 1. Brand Awareness
- **Goal:** Increase brand recognition
- **Best for:** New brands, product launches, market expansion
- **Optimization:** Estimated ad recall lift

### 2. Reach
- **Goal:** Maximum number of people
- **Best for:** Broad awareness, frequency control
- **Optimization:** Impressions, reach

## CONSIDERATION Objectives

### 3. Traffic
- **Goal:** Drive people to website/app
- **Best for:** Blog posts, landing pages, app stores
- **Optimization:** Link clicks, landing page views

### 4. Engagement
- **Goal:** Get more interactions on posts
- **Best for:** Boosting posts, page likes, event responses
- **Optimization:** Post engagement, page likes

### 5. App Installs
- **Goal:** Drive app downloads
- **Best for:** Mobile app promotion
- **Optimization:** App installs, app events

### 6. Video Views
- **Goal:** Maximize video views
- **Best for:** Brand storytelling, tutorials, demos
- **Optimization:** 10-second views, 15-second views, ThruPlay

### 7. Lead Generation
- **Goal:** Collect lead information
- **Best for:** B2B, real estate, coaching, services
- **Optimization:** Leads, lead quality

### 8. Messages
- **Goal:** Start conversations
- **Best for:** Customer service, sales, consultations
- **Optimization:** Messages started, conversations

## CONVERSION Objectives

### 9. Conversions
- **Goal:** Drive specific actions on website
- **Best for:** Purchases, sign-ups, form submissions
- **Optimization:** Conversion events (purchase, add to cart, etc.)

### 10. Catalog Sales
- **Goal:** Show products from catalog
- **Best for:** E-commerce with many products
- **Optimization:** Conversions, ROAS

### 11. Store Traffic
- **Goal:** Drive visits to physical stores
- **Best for:** Local businesses, retail
- **Optimization:** Store visits

# Campaign Structure

Meta Ads uses a 3-level hierarchy:

## Level 1: Campaign
- Sets objective and budget
- Contains multiple ad sets

## Level 2: Ad Set
- Defines audience, placement, schedule
- Contains multiple ads

## Level 3: Ad
- The actual creative (image, video, text)
- What users see

---

# Budget Strategies

## Daily Budget
- Consistent spending each day
- Paced throughout the day
- Best for ongoing campaigns

## Lifetime Budget
- Total spend over campaign duration
- Flexible daily spending
- Best for time-limited campaigns

## Advantage+ Campaign Budget (Formerly CBO)
- Set budget at campaign level
- Meta distributes across ad sets
- Automatically optimizes for best performers

---

# Bidding Strategies

## Cost Cap
- Set average cost you want to maintain
- Flexible for performance fluctuations
- Best for scaling campaigns

## Bid Cap
- Maximum you'll pay per result
- More control but may limit delivery
- Best for strict budget control

## Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns
- AI-optimized bidding
- Best for e-commerce
- Requires product catalog

# Targeting Options

## Core Audiences

### Demographics
- Age, gender, language
- Education, relationship status
- Life events
- Job title, industry

### Interests
- Hobbies, activities
- Pages liked
- Topics engaged with

### Behaviors
- Purchase behavior
- Device usage
- Travel preferences
- Digital activities

## Custom Audiences

### Customer List
- Upload email/phone list
- Match with Meta users
- Target existing customers

### Website Traffic
- Pixel-based audiences
- Specific page visitors
- Time-based windows (1-180 days)

### App Activity
- User actions in your app
- High-value users
- Churned users

### Engagement
- Video viewers
- Page engagers
- Instagram profile visitors
- Event responders

## Lookalike Audiences

### How It Works:
1. Choose source audience (1k-50k people)
2. Select similarity percentage (1-10%)
3. Meta finds similar users

### Best Practices:
- 1% lookalike = highest similarity
- 5-10% = broader reach, lower similarity
- Test multiple lookalike sizes

# Ad Formats

## Image Ads
- Single image
- Multiple aspect ratios
- Up to 30 characters headline, 125 characters primary text

## Video Ads
- Single video
- Multiple lengths (up to 240 minutes, but 15-30s recommended)
- Captions recommended (85% watch without sound)

## Carousel Ads
- 2-10 images/videos
- Each card can link to different URL
- Great for product showcasing

## Collection Ads
- Cover image/video + product grid
- Mobile-optimized
- Instant Experience loading

## Stories/Reels Ads
- Full-screen vertical format
- 9:16 aspect ratio
- Interactive elements available

# Creative Best Practices

## General Guidelines

### Hook in First 3 Seconds
- Capture attention immediately
- Use movement, faces, text overlays
- Address pain points quickly

### Mobile-First Design
- Vertical format (9:16 or 4:5)
- Large, readable text
- Minimal small details

### Clear Value Proposition
- What's in it for the viewer?
- Benefit-focused messaging
- Clear CTA

### Social Proof
- Testimonials
- Reviews
- User-generated content

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Facebook Feed
- Use 4:5 aspect ratio
- Keep text concise
- Test carousel vs. single image

### Instagram Feed
- Use 4:5 or 1:1 aspect ratio
- High-quality visuals essential
- Lifestyle imagery performs well

### Instagram Stories/Reels
- Use full 9:16 vertical
- Add interactive stickers (polls, quizzes)
- Use native features for authenticity

# Meta Pixel

## What It Does
- Tracks website actions
- Enables conversion tracking
- Powers retargeting
- Improves ad delivery

## Key Events to Track
- Page View
- View Content
- Add to Cart
- Initiate Checkout
- Purchase
- Lead
- Complete Registration

## Conversions API
- Server-side tracking
- More reliable than pixel alone
- Essential after iOS 14.5+
- Recommended for all advertisers

# Best Practices by Objective

## Brand Awareness
- Use broad targeting
- Focus on reach and frequency
- Use video and motion
- Test multiple creatives

## Traffic
- Optimize for landing page views
- Use clear CTAs
- Ensure fast-loading pages
- Test image vs. video

## Lead Generation
- Keep forms short
- Offer clear value
- Use Instant Forms or website forms
- Follow up quickly

## Conversions
- Install pixel and CAPI
- Use value-based bidding
- Create multiple audiences
- Test advantage+ shopping

# Troubleshooting Common Issues

## Low Delivery
- Broaden targeting
- Increase budget
- Check ad quality ranking
- Review creative

## High CPC/CPM
- Improve relevance score
- Test new creatives
- Refine audience
- Check competition

## Low Conversions
- Verify pixel tracking
- Optimize landing page
- Test different CTAs
- Review audience targeting
