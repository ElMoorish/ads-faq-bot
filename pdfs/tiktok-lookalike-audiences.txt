# About Lookalike Audiences | TikTok Ads Manager
## Official Documentation

---

Lookalike audiences can help you reach audiences that share commonalities with your existing customers, and deliver your ads to audiences who are interested in your product.

---

## How It Works

The model uses algorithms to:
1. Study the attributes of your custom audience
2. Look for users who are similar
3. Target users with potential to improve performance

---

## Audience Size Options

| Size | Similarity | Reach | Best For |
|------|------------|-------|----------|
| **Narrow** | Highest | Smallest | Precise targeting, high intent |
| **Balanced** | Moderate | Moderate | Balanced approach |
| **Broad** | Lower | Largest | Scale, awareness |

### Narrow Lookalike
- ✅ Highest similarity to source audience
- ❌ Smaller audience size
- **Best for:** High-intent campaigns, precise targeting

### Balanced Lookalike
- ✅ Moderate similarity
- ✅ Moderate reach
- **Best for:** Balanced performance

### Broad Lookalike
- ✅ Largest reach
- ❌ Lower similarity
- **Best for:** Scale, awareness campaigns

---

## Requirements

### Minimum Source Audience
- **Minimum:** 1,000 people in source audience

### Audience Size Factors
The size of the lookalike audience is based on:
- User base of the market specified
- Scale selected (Narrow/Balanced/Broad)

---

## Best Practices

1. **Start with Narrow** if you want highest similarity
2. **Test multiple sizes** to find what works
3. **Use high-quality source audience** (purchasers work best)
4. **Combine with other targeting** for better results
5. **Refresh periodically** as source audience changes

---

## Source Audience Options

Create lookalikes from:
- Customer lists
- Website visitors (pixel data)
- App users
- Engaged users (video views, profile visits)
