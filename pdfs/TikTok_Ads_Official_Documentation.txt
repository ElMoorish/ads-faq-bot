# Getting Started with TikTok Ads Manager

## Why Advertise on TikTok?

TikTok has been one of the world's most downloaded apps, with over a billion people worldwide using it to be entertained, inspired, educated, and shop.

**Key Benefits:**
- **Worldwide Reach** - Over 1 billion users globally
- **Effective Engagement** - Fun, imaginative format inspires participation
- **Creative Ad Formats** - Templates and tools for creating engaging ads
- **Powerful Campaign Management** - Create, launch, and measure with ease
- **Outcome-based Objectives** - Awareness, Consideration, Conversion goals
- **Precise Targeting** - Gender, location, age, interests, behaviors
- **Holistic Measurement** - Detailed dashboards and customized reports

# Creating Your TikTok Ads Manager Account

## Step 1: Sign Up
1. Go to TikTok Ads Manager signup page: https://ads.tiktok.com/i18n/signup
2. Fill in email address or phone number and password
3. Provide business information:
   - Country/region
   - Time zone (cannot be changed later!)
   - Preferred currency for billing
   - Legal business name
   - Industry

## Step 2: Choose Campaign Objective

Your campaign objective guides what action you want viewers to take.

### Three Objective Categories:

**AWARENESS**
- Reach - Maximum exposure to broad audience
- Brand Awareness - Increase brand recognition

**CONSIDERATION**
- Traffic - Drive people to website/app
- App Installs - Promote app downloads
- Video Views - Maximize video views
- Lead Generation - Collect lead information
- Community Interaction - Increase profile visits

**CONVERSION**
- Conversions - Drive specific actions on website
- Product Sales - Promote products
- Shop Purchases - Drive TikTok Shop sales

# Targeting Options

## Targeting Variables:
- **Location** - Countries, regions, cities
- **Demographics** - Age, gender, language
- **Interests** - Categories and topics
- **Behaviors** - Purchase behavior, device usage
- **Device** - iOS, Android, specific devices

## Best Practice: Broad Targeting
For most advertisers, broad targeting is recommended as a starting point. Automatic targeting is integrated into all broad ads, making it highly effective.

Even sophisticated advertisers should test to ensure advanced techniques outperform broad targeting.

# Bidding Methods

TikTok offers four bidding methods:

## 1. CPM (Cost Per Mille/Thousand Impressions)
- **What it is:** Price you pay for 1,000 impressions
- **When to use:** Brand awareness, reach campaigns
- **Billing:** Charged by impression
- **Objectives:** Reach

## 2. oCPM (Optimized CPM)
- **What it is:** Pay for 1,000 impressions targeting people likely to take action
- **When to use:** Conversions, app installs, lead generation
- **Billing:** Charged by impression
- **Objectives:** Shop purchases, Product sales, Conversions, App install, Lead generation, Community interaction

## 3. CPV (Cost Per View)
- **What it is:** Pay when someone:
  - Views ad for at least 6 seconds
  - Interacts with ad within first 6 seconds
- **When to use:** Video views campaigns
- **Billing:** Charged per focused view
- **Objectives:** Video views

## 4. CPC (Cost Per Click)
- **What it is:** Price per click
- **When to use:** Traffic, conversions, app installs
- **Billing:** Charged per click
- **Objectives:** Shop purchases, Product sales, Traffic, Conversions, App install, Community interaction

# Budget Types

## Daily Budget
- Specific amount spent per day
- Can be changed anytime
- System paces spending throughout the day

## Lifetime Budget
- Total amount for campaign duration
- System automatically paces spending
- Flexible for uneven spending patterns

# TikTok Ad Specifications

## Video Ads
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16, 1:1, 16:9
- **Resolution:** 720x1280 or higher
- **Length:** 5-60 seconds (15-30s recommended)
- **Format:** MP4, MOV
- **File Size:** Up to 500MB

## Image Ads
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 recommended
- **Resolution:** 1080x1080 minimum
- **Format:** JPG, PNG
- **File Size:** Up to 50MB

# Best Practices

## Creative Best Practices:
1. **Hook in first 3 seconds** - Capture attention immediately
2. **Native feel** - Ads should feel like organic TikTok content
3. **Vertical format** - Use 9:16 for full-screen impact
4. **Sound on** - Use trending sounds and music
5. **Clear CTA** - Tell viewers what to do next

## Targeting Best Practices:
1. Start with broad targeting
2. Test advanced targeting vs. broad
3. Use lookalike audiences after pixel data collected
4. Exclude irrelevant audiences

## Budget Best Practices:
1. Start with daily budget for control
2. Allow 2-3 days for algorithm learning
3. Don't change budget more than 20% at once
4. Scale winners, cut losers quickly
