# About Advantage+ Audience | Meta Ads
## Meta Business Help Center

---

Meta Advantage+ audience lets you use Meta's advanced AI to find your campaign audience. It can be the **easiest to use and best-performing** Advantage+ audience automation option yet.

---

## Performance Improvements

| Objective | Cost Per Result Improvement |
|-----------|----------------------------|
| **Awareness** | **-14.8%** lower |
| **Traffic, Engagement, Leads** | **-9.7%** lower |
| **Sales, App Promotion** | **-7.2%** lower |

---

## How Advantage+ Audience Works

Meta's AI uses numerous pieces of information to find your audience:

| Signal | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Past conversions** | Who converted before |
| **Meta pixel data** | Website behavior |
| **Ad interactions** | How people engaged with previous ads |
| **Millions of signals** | Constantly learning and evolving |

**Latest model innovations** analyze millions of signals to find the right message, for the right audience, at the right time.

---

## How Suggestions Work

1. Meta's AI finds your audience OR
2. Adds information as an **audience suggestion**
3. Shows ads to people matching suggestion AND other audiences
4. Expands when likely to improve performance

---

## When to Use Advantage+ Audience

### ✅ Recommended
- Almost all campaign types
- Sales, Lead, App promotion objectives
- When you want AI to find your audience

### ❌ NOT Recommended
- Remarketing campaigns

---

## For Sales, Lead, App Promotion Objectives

**Better option:** **Advantage+ campaigns**

- More complete campaign solution
- Applies AI across many steps
- Full automation

**Alternative:** Advantage+ audience
- AI finds audience
- You still control other settings

---

## Testing Best Practice

Use **A/B testing** to compare Advantage+ audience against other audience selections.

**Important:** This is the only reliable way to compare two audience sets.

---

## Limitations

- Cannot select ages under 18 (or older in some countries)
- Not for remarketing campaigns

---

## Opportunity Score

When eligible, you get Advantage+ audience recommendations in **opportunity score**.

- Tool to identify and prioritize effective campaign recommendations
- Near real-time feedback

**Note:** Opportunity score does NOT guarantee performance.
