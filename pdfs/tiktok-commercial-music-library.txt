# About the Commercial Music Library
## TikTok Ads Official Documentation

---

The TikTok Commercial Music Library (CML) is a **pre-cleared global music library of 1 million songs** that helps make it easier for businesses to find music to soundtrack their content on TikTok.

---

## What's Included

- **1 million songs**
- All styles and genres
- All regions
- Sourced from:
  - Independent artists
  - Emerging artists
  - Top-tier music houses

**Cost:** FREE for businesses to use

---

## Important: Business vs Personal Music

### ❌ Businesses CANNOT Use
- General TikTok music library
- For commercial usage

### ✅ Businesses SHOULD Use
- Commercial Music Library (CML)
- For ALL commercial TikTok activities

---

## Approved Commercial Uses

| Use Case | Allowed |
|----------|---------|
| **Organic content** | ✅ Yes |
| Duet with user videos | ✅ Yes |
| React to user videos | ✅ Yes |
| Stitch with user videos | ✅ Yes |
| **Video ads** | ✅ Yes |
| **Branded content** | ✅ Yes |

---

## Legal Warning

If you choose to use:
- Original sound from other users
- Other licensed sounds

**You must:** Consult your legal team to ensure you have obtained a proper license.

---

## Quick Reference

```
Business Account → Use CML Only = Safe & Legal
Business Account → Use General Music = Risk of Copyright Issues
```

---

## Access

- Available in TikTok Ads Manager
- Available in TikTok Business Suite
- Search by genre, mood, tempo, etc.
