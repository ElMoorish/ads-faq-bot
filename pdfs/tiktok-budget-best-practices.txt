# Budget Best Practices | TikTok Ads Manager
## Official Documentation

---

Your ad budget can make up a large portion of your marketing spending. Use these tips to help you consider the best budget and strategy when you set up a campaign.

---

## Key Budget Tips

### 1. Campaign Budget
- ✅ **Leave it open initially** (don't set a hard cap right away)

### 2. Ad Group Budget
- ✅ **Use daily budget** instead of lifetime budget
- **Why:** Better control and pacing

---

## Minimum Budget Recommendations

| Event Type | Optimization | Minimum Budget |
|------------|--------------|----------------|
| No "Current CPA" | Not Conversions, any high funnel event | 50 × current CPA/CPR OR $100 |
| Conversions | Shallow Conversion (Page View, etc.) | 50 × current CPA/CPR OR $100 |
| Conversions | Install Campaign | 20 × current CPA OR $200 |
| Conversions | Deep Conversion (Add to Cart, Purchase) | 10 × current CPA OR $200 |

---

## Learning Phase Rules

### During Learning Phase (Conversions Campaign)
- ❌ **DO NOT** change targeting
- ❌ **DO NOT** change bid
- ❌ **DO NOT** change budget
- ⏳ Wait for learning phase to complete

### After Learning Phase
- ✅ Keep budget within **50% range** of previous budget
- ✅ Wait **at least 2 days** between changes
- ✅ Allow campaign time to adjust

---

## Maximum Delivery Notes

If you set up or change a Maximum Delivery bid strategy in the middle of the day:
- ⚠️ There might be a spending discount
- Based on actual remaining effective hours of delivery

---

## Quick Checklist

### ✅ Setup Phase
- [ ] Leave campaign budget open initially
- [ ] Use daily budget for ad groups
- [ ] Set minimum budget based on optimization type

### ✅ Learning Phase
- [ ] No targeting changes
- [ ] No bid changes
- [ ] No budget changes
- [ ] Wait for completion

### ✅ Post-Learning
- [ ] Changes within 50% of previous budget
- [ ] Wait 2+ days between changes
- [ ] Monitor performance
