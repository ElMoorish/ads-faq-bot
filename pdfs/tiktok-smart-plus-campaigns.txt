## Features

### Simplified Setup
TikTok offers one simple and easy interface that guides you to success with minimal effort.

**You only need to provide:**
- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Creative assets
- Target geography
- Target language

**Smart+ handles:**
- Targeting
- Optimization
- Bidding
- Creative rotation

### Optimize Ad Management
TikTok helps you create and manage high-performing ads based on your business goals. Your campaigns benefit from all best practices built into Smart+ right out of the box.

### Paid and Organic Optimization
- Automatically add an organic anchor link to select posts
- Drive conversions
- Maximize impact across TikTok
- **Note:** Anchor links only visible to users aged 18+

### Automated Creative Generation
TikTok's automated creative solution helps optimize ad creative combinations:
- CTAs
- Format variations
- Ad group fatigue detection
- Auto-refresh strategies

**Benefits:**
- Reduces ad fatigue
- Prolongs ad group lifespan
- Improves cost efficiency over time
- Shifts budget towards highest-performing creative
- Pauses poor performers

### Advanced Creative AI Features
Optional creative AI features include:
- Adding and remixing various elements
- Music
- Hooks
- Formats
- Scripts
- Generate and deliver multiple variations of ads

**Note:** You can turn off Smart+ generated creatives in TikTok Ads Manager at any time.
