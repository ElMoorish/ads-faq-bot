# About GMV Max Campaigns | TikTok Ads Manager
## Official Documentation

---

GMV Max is an automated ad campaign type that optimizes for **total channel ROI** for your TikTok Shop.

---

## Campaign Types

| Type | Purpose | Best For |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Product GMV Max** | Auto-select products, creatives, placements for sales | E-commerce, product sales |
| **LIVE GMV Max** | Optimize liveroom traffic for highest revenue | Live sellers, TikTok Shop LIVE |

---

## Key Features

### 1. Maximize Your GMV
- Automate ad creative selection
- Automate audience settings
- Automate placement options
- Uses signals from both organic AND paid traffic

### 2. Optimize Your Ad Budget
- Choose your ROI target
- Campaign automatically spends within target's range

### 3. Product-Generated Creative
- Use existing product descriptions
- Use product videos
- Use product listings
- Auto-generate content for ads

### 4. Simplified Campaign Creation
- Create in TikTok Ads Manager
- Manage permissions
- Add content for Spark Ads
- Familiar interface

---

## How It Works

---

## Requirements

- TikTok Shop account
- Product catalog/listings
- Creative assets (optional - can use product content)

---

## Quick Setup

1. Go to TikTok Ads Manager
2. Create new campaign
3. Select GMV Max objective
4. Choose Product or LIVE GMV Max
5. Set ROI target
6. Add products/liveroom
7. Launch
