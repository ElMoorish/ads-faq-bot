# Ad Targeting Dimensions | TikTok Ads Manager
## Official Documentation

---

Ad targeting allows you to deliver ads to specific people based on:

---

## Targeting Dimensions

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Demographics** | Age, gender, language |
| **Audience Targeting** | Custom audiences, lookalikes |
| **Advanced Targeting** | Interests, behaviors |
| **Device Type** | iOS, Android, specific devices |
| **Smart Targeting** | AI-expanded targeting |

---

## How Targeting Logic Works

### Within Same Dimension = OR Logic
Selections within the same dimension operate on **OR logic**.

**Example:** If you select Interest Beauty OR #makeup within Interest and Behavior:
- TikTok includes people who meet **EITHER** criteria

### Across Dimensions = AND Logic
Selections across dimensions operate with **AND logic**.

**Example:** If you select Female AND 25-44:
- TikTok only includes users that meet **BOTH** criteria

---

## Where to Configure

Targeting settings are configured at the **ad group level** during campaign creation.

---

## Important Notes

- **Available targeting locations vary by market**
- Some targeting settings may not be available for age-restricted products/services
- Special rules apply when advertising to people under 18
