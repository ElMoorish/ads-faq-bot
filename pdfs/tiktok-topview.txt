# About TopView | TikTok Ads
## Official Documentation

---

TopView is a video ad that is shown to users **upon opening TikTok**. Shown before any other TikTok video, TopView is a high-impact, full-screen, and sound-on video that showcases your brand.

---

## How TopView Works

### The Experience

1. **User opens TikTok**
2. **First 3 seconds:** Full-screen video takeover (no UI elements)
3. **After 3 seconds:** Transitions to in-feed video experience
   - Icons appear
   - Interaction elements appear

## Key Features

### Premium Position
- **"Times Square billboard in a user's pocket"**
- First video users see when opening TikTok
- Maximum visibility and impact

### Guaranteed Impressions
- Impressions are **100% guaranteed**
- "Own the day" by buying out TopView impressions
- Reach vast numbers within 24-hour period

### Buying Model
- **CPM basis** (Cost Per Mille)
- Customizable audience targeting
- Minimum spend varies by market
- Fixed rate (via sales rep) OR dynamic CPM (via Campaign Forecaster)
