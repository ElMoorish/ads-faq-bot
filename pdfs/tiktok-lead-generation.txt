# About Lead Generation Objective | TikTok Ads Manager
## Official Documentation

---

Lead generation is an advertising objective that lets you cultivate prospects and engage leads on TikTok or collect lead information on your own website.

---

## How It Works

When someone taps the CTA button on a lead ad:

1. **Instant Form** opens
   - Learn about your business
   - Leave contact information
   - Signal interest in product/service

2. **Redirect Options:**
   - Your website for contact info
   - TikTok Direct Messages
   - Popular instant messaging apps

---

## Benefits of Instant Forms

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Faster conversions** | Forms load faster than typical landing pages |
| **Pre-populated fields** | Auto-fill based on available information |
| **Higher conversion rate** | Frictionless experience |
| **Customizable design** | Your messaging and layout |
| **Web traffic** | Redirect after form submission |
| **CRM integration** | Sync with Zapier, LeadsBridge, Custom APIs |

---

## When to Use Your Own Website Form

- ✅ Boost traffic to your own site
- ✅ Qualify leads via existing logic on your site
- ✅ Ask detailed qualifying questions not supported by TikTok instant form

## Best Practices

### Campaign Structure
- Launch with **3-5 ad groups**
- Different targeting settings per ad group
- Different creative per ad group

### Budget
- Daily budget = **10x your expected CPA**

### Targeting
- Start with **broadly defined audiences**
- Analyze performance to refine

### Integration
- Connect TikTok Leads to CRM
- Automate marketing funnel

### Lead Forecast Tool
Use the lead forecast tool to estimate daily leads based on:
- Optimization location: Instant Form or website
- Budget: Daily budget
- Bidding: Maximum delivery
- Targeting: Broad audience

**Access:** Right navigation of TikTok Ads Manager when editing ad group settings
