# About Catalog Ads | TikTok Ads Manager
## Official Documentation

---

Catalog Ads are an ad solution that uses images, videos, and product information from your Catalog to create personalized ads.

---

## How It Works

When someone sees your Catalog Ad:
1. They can visit your website or Instant Page
2. Learn more about products
3. Make a purchase

---

## Availability

**Objectives:** Sales objective for Website, App, or Smart+

---

## Key Benefits

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Enhanced personalization** | ML algorithms match most relevant products to users |
| **Higher engagement** | Relevant products increase conversion likelihood |
| **Smart+ Catalog Ads** | Automation + catalog = better CPA vs manual campaigns |

---

## Ad Formats

| Format | Description | Best For |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Carousel** | Connect catalog directly to ad creative | Always-on sales, prospecting, retargeting |
| **Single Video** | Upload/choose videos with interactive add-ons | Product showcases |
| **Catalog Video** | Auto-present videos aligned with user interest | High engagement, relevance |

---

## Quick Setup

1. Create/upload catalog in TikTok Ads Manager
2. Set up data connections (product feed)
3. Create Sales campaign
4. Select Catalog Ad format
5. Choose products to promote
6. Launch

---

## Requirements

- Product catalog with images/videos
- Product information (descriptions, prices)
- Data feed or API connection
